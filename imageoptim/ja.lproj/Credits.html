イメージオプティム by コルネル Lesiński and contributors is a GUI for 3rd party utilities:

  • OptiPNG by Cosmin Truta,
  • PNGCrush by Glenn Randers-Pehrson,
  • MozJPEG by Mozilla and contributors,
  • Zopfli by Jyrki Alakuijala, Lode Vandevenne,
  • AdvPNG by Andrea Mazzoleni, Filipe Estima,
  • Jpegoptim by Timo Kokkonen,
  • Gifsicle by Eddie Kohler,
  • SVGO by Kir Belevich,
  • and PNGOUT by Ken Silverman.
